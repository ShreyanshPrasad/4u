Who We Are

A Movement Built on
Compassion & Action

4U – Use Unity to Uplift Universe is a grassroots social initiative committed to serving underprivileged communities. From cleanliness drives and food distribution to visiting orphanages and supporting the elderly, we believe small acts of kindness can create massive waves of change.

🤝
Community-first approach to social upliftment
💚

🌍

What We Do

Our Core Initiatives

Every initiative is a step toward a more equal, clean, and compassionate world. Here's how we're making a difference every single day.

🧹

Cleanliness Drives

Organizing neighborhood clean-up drives to create hygienic, beautiful public spaces. We've cleaned parks, roads, and water bodies across multiple districts.

Learn more →
🏠

Orphanage Visits

Regular visits to orphanages with food, supplies, and emotional support. We aim to bring smiles to children who need love and attention the most.

Learn more →
🧓

Old Age Home Support

Spending quality time with the elderly, providing essentials, and ensuring they feel valued and connected to the community they helped build.

Learn more →
🍱

Food Distribution

Distributing nutritious meals to the homeless, daily-wage workers, and families in need. No one should go to bed hungry — and we mean it.

Learn more →
