Every rupee you contribute goes directly to the ground — no middlemen, no administrative overhead. Here's exactly what your support funds.

You Can Trust Us

Our Commitment to Transparency

We understand that trust must be earned. Here's how we ensure every donation is used responsibly and makes the maximum possible impact.

📊

100% Goes to the Ground

We are a fully volunteer-run initiative. There is no paid staff and no administrative overhead. Every rupee you donate goes directly to funding an initiative, not an office.

📢

Public Impact Reporting

After every event, we share photos, outcomes, and spending on our social media. Donors can see exactly what their contribution funded — people, supplies, and results.

🤝

Community-First Decisions

Every spending decision is made collectively by our core team based on what communities tell us they need. Donors are never pressured and can choose which initiative their funds support.
