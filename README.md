# 4U – Use Unity to Uplift Universe

## Overview

4U – Use Unity to Uplift Universe is a grassroots social initiative committed to serving underprivileged communities through consistent, compassionate, and community-led action. Founded in 2021, we believe that small acts of kindness, when multiplied through unity, can create massive waves of positive change.

Our mission is to serve underprivileged communities through cleanliness drives, food distribution, orphanage visits, and elder care support — in a way that preserves dignity, builds relationships, and creates lasting positive change at the grassroots level.

## Our Story

It all started with a simple observation: people in our own neighborhoods going to bed hungry, elderly citizens forgotten by society, and children in orphanages starved of attention. Instead of looking away, a small group of like-minded individuals decided to act.

What began as informal conversations around a table has grown into a movement of over 500 volunteers across 25+ areas, having directly impacted more than 8,000 lives through 120+ events. We operate with zero bureaucracy and 100% heart, showing up wherever we're needed.

## Core Initiatives

### 🧹 Cleanliness Drives
We organize neighborhood clean-up drives to create hygienic, beautiful public spaces. Our volunteers have transformed neglected parks, roads, and water bodies across multiple districts, fostering community pride and environmental responsibility.

### 🏠 Orphanage Visits
Regular visits to orphanages with food, supplies, and emotional support. We aim to bring smiles to children who need love and attention the most, celebrating festivals and creating joyful memories.

### 🧓 Old Age Home Support
Spending quality time with the elderly, providing essentials, and ensuring they feel valued and connected to the community they helped build. We listen to their stories and provide the companionship that combats loneliness.

### 🍱 Food Distribution
Distributing nutritious meals to the homeless, daily-wage workers, and families in need. We partner with local communities to ensure no one goes to bed hungry, with a focus on dignity and sustainability.

## Design System

The website uses a consistent design system with:
- **Color Palette**: Blue (#0f2460, #1E3A8A), Green (#16A34A, #22c55e), and neutral grays
- **Typography**: Playfair Display for headings, Poppins for body text
- **Components**: Buttons, cards, sections with consistent spacing and animations
- **Responsive**: Mobile-first design that works on all devices

## How to View the Website

Since this is a static website, you can view it by:

1. **Local Development**:
